RdSAP 10: A New Era of Accuracy

The most significant update to domestic energy assessments in over a decade is here. RdSAP 10 shifts the focus from broad assumptions to a detailed, evidence-based approach. This guide visualizes what you need to know.

From Assumption to Evidence

Previously, EPCs relied heavily on default values due to software limitations. RdSAP 10 demands a more granular look, requiring specific measurements and documented proof to build a true picture of a property's energy performance. This change empowers accurate, reliable assessments.

The On-Site Data Revolution

The new methodology requires assessors to collect significantly more data on-site. Here are the key areas of change where detailed observation is now mandatory.
